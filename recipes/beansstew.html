Beans-Stew

Beans stew and diced plantain

Ingredients 2 Pieces Cameroon pepper 2 Tablespoons Red pepper flakes(ground pepper/ata gigun) 2.5 Oz Dried bell pepper (or shobo or mixture) 1 Cup Palm Oil (bleached) or more 1 Onion (sliced or diced) 2 Tablespoons Ground Crayfish and fish (you can use just ground crayfish) 1/2 Cup Grilled fish (optional) Handful small dried fish (optional); soaked in hot water and salt. Seasoning Cube/powder Salt to taste
